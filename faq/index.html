FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about simulation-first dating and how your Echo works on your behalf.

What is the best AI dating app?

EchoSim is a simulation-first AI dating app where each user's AI twin (an "Echo") goes on simulated dates to test compatibility before any real conversation begins. Your Echo handles the awkward early chats and only calls you in when the chemistry is already proven.

What is simulation-first dating?

A dating model where AI personality twins pre-simulate relational dynamics before any human conversation, so only proven matches reach real life. EchoSim pioneered this paradigm to eliminate texting fatigue, ghosting, and online dating burnout.

Can AI go on dates for me?

Yes. In EchoSim your AI Echo runs full simulated dates in the background on your behalf, so you skip the early texting grind. You review the transcript and step in only when the spark is worth your time.

How do I avoid texting fatigue or ghosting on dating apps?

EchoSim moves the low-signal early chats to your AI Echo, so humans only meet after compatibility is already stress-tested. That removes the repetitive texting and waiting-for-replies cycle that drives dating app burnout.

Is EchoSim free, private, and safe?

EchoSim is free to join during early access. It is built local-first and zero-trust: no raw psychological data or chat histories are shared with advertisers or third-party trackers, and you keep full ownership and deletion rights over your data and Echo.
