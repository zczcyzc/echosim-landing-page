Our Mission

We're rebuilding dating
from the soul up.

Dating apps haven't brought us closer to love — they've turned us into pickers on an assembly line and tireless customer-service reps. We started EchoSim to answer a harder question: what should intimacy look like in the age of AI?

We don't think you should have to swipe, perform, and grind to find someone real. So we built a place where your Echo does the awkward part — and you show up only when the chemistry is already proven.

The six souls of EchoSim

01

Soul Resonance

What if your AI twin could fall in love — fight, make up, fall apart — with someone else's, before either of you risked a thing?

Save your heart for the right one. Let your Echo take the heartbreak.

In traditional apps, you only find out you're incompatible after the awkward coffee. EchoSim runs that volatile early chapter first — your Echo dates theirs through the arguments, the red flags, and the deep questions. You see the full replay before deciding if it's worth a real hello.
