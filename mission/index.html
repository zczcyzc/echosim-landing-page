Our Mission

We're rebuilding dating
from the soul up.

Dating apps haven't brought us closer to love — they've turned us into pickers on an assembly line and tireless customer-service reps. We started EchoSim to answer a harder question: what should intimacy look like in the age of AI?

We don't think you should have to swipe, perform, and grind to find someone real. So we built a place where your Echo does the awkward part — and you show up only when the chemistry is already proven.

The six souls of EchoSim

01

Soul Resonance

What if your AI twin could fall in love — fight, make up, fall apart — with someone else's, before either of you risked a thing?

Save your heart for the right one. Let your Echo take the heartbreak.

In traditional apps, proving you're right for each other is expensive — match, small talk, meet, then fall apart over one tiny clash of values. EchoSim runs that volatile early chapter first. Your Echo dates theirs through the arguments, the red flags, and the deep questions, so only the ones who truly survive the test ever reach your real life.

The Persona Filter

Photos are retouched, bios are polished, even the texts are AI-written. So who are they, really?

Anyone can fake a perfect profile. No one can fake a reaction.

A static profile is a pruned bonsai; real character shows in how someone reacts under pressure. Our simulator is a stress test — thrown into an unscripted crisis with no time to perform, people reveal the values underneath. We don't read how they brand themselves. We watch how they choose.
