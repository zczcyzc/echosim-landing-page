Coins

A comprehensive guide to EchoSim's coin-based matching economy, simulation resets, and premium features.

By the EchoSim Team · Last updated June 1, 2026

In short: Coins are EchoSim's in-app tokens that power your Echo's background date simulations. Every user gets a free daily allowance (Daily Expired Coins, spent first and reset every 24 hours); purchased Ordinary Coins never expire and are used only once your daily allowance runs out. Early access registrants get 500 free Coins.

To power background pre-simulations and prevent spam or low-effort match harvesting, EchoSim implements a local token economy powered by Coins.

1. What are Coins used for?

Coins are used to fuel your Echo's AI compute resources for background sandboxed date simulations:

  • Initiating Simulations: When you match with another user, running the initial background simulated date consumes a small, standard amount of coins.
  • Deeper Scenario Resets: If you want to stress-test compatibility under new conditions (e.g., a simulation exploring financial conflicts or moving in together), you can reset the sandbox with new scenarios by spending coins.
  • Advanced Analytics: Unlocking deeper compatibility breakdowns or extended dialogue analyses uses coin triggers.

2. How do I get Coins?

We believe dating shouldn't be a financial grind. You can acquire coins in multiple ways:

  • Daily Allowances: Every active EchoSim user receives a set amount of free Coins every single day.
  • In-App Purchases (IAP): If you are eager to run dozens of background simulations in a single day, you can buy coin bundles directly through Apple or Google billing.
  • Early Access Boost: During our beta and early access phase, all registrants receive a massive starting bundle of 500 free Coins.

3. Daily Expired Coins vs. Ordinary Coins

EchoSim uses a two-tier token system to ensure all active users can date daily for free, while allowing heavy users to run continuous simulations:

✨ Daily Expired Coins (Free Daily Allowance)
  • Refill & Expiry: Automatically refreshed every 24 hours. They do not roll over or accumulate past your daily allowance cap (they reset and expire daily).
  • Deduction Priority: The system always prioritizes consuming your Daily Expired Coins first.
🪙 Ordinary Coins (Purchased / Permanent Coins)
  • Expiry: Acquired through in-app purchases or early access boosts. They never expire and remain in your wallet permanently until used.
  • Usage: Automatically consumed only when your Daily Expired Coins are fully depleted.
