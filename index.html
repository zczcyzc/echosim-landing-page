Features

Technology

AI that understands
how people actually connect.

🧬

Personality Engine

Each Echo is modeled on MBTI type, attachment style, emotional labor tendency, and intent spectrum. Behavior emerges from the intersection of all four — not a script.

⚡

LLM-Powered Simulation

Date conversations are generated by large language models with structured psychology prompts. Every simulation is unique; no two dates play out the same way.

📈

Real-time Affection Scoring

A proprietary scoring engine tracks chemistry moment-by-moment throughout the date, producing an affection curve and compatibility verdict at the end.

🔒

Identity Verification

Liveness detection and face verification ensure every profile behind an Echo is a real person — no bots, no catfishing.
