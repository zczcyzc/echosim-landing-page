DMCA Copyright Complaint

EchoSim · Pursuant to 17 U.S.C. §512

EchoSim respects intellectual property rights. If you believe content on our platform infringes your copyright, please complete the form below or email legal@echosim.ai.

What to include

A valid DMCA takedown notice must include:

  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed.
  • The URL or specific location of the allegedly infringing content on EchoSim.
  • Your contact information (name and email).
  • A good faith statement that you believe the use is not authorized.
  • A statement that the information in the notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf.

Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice to legal@echosim.ai. A counter-notice must include:

  • Identification of the removed content and where it appeared before removal.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe the material was removed by mistake or misidentification.
  • Your name, address, phone number, and consent to jurisdiction of the federal court in your district.
